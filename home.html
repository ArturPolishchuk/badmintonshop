WHAT IS BADMINTON?

Badminton is a fast-paced, dynamic sport played with a shuttlecock and rackets. It can be played both indoors and outdoors, and involves quick reflexes, agility, and strategy. Players engage in singles or doubles matches, aiming to score points by hitting the shuttlecock over a net and into the opponent’s court. The game is known for its high energy and can be enjoyed by people of all skill levels, from beginners to experts.
